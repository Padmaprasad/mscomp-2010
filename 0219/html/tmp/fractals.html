Fractals¶

A simple ilustration of fractal curve it the von Koch curve K.

Wich is constructed by the procedure mentioned below: To form the iteration we take a line segment of longitud L. The segment is splitted in three equal parts and the middle one is substracted. Then we replace the missing segment with the other two sides of an equlateral triangle.

Subsecuently iteration consist in repeat the procedure for each one or the segments in the previous approximation.

In this exercise, you are asked to plot the six first iteration in the construction of the von Koch curve as ilustrated in the figure.
